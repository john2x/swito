`swito` answers simple questions I find myself asking semi-frequently.

# Percentage questions
```
$ swito 10% of 100
10% of 100 is 10

$ swito 30/90 in %
30 is 33.3333% of 90

$ swito 100 to 30 in %
100 to 30 is a -70% change
```
